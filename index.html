About Mabel Pines

Mabel Pines and the other chatbots (Pacifica and Dipper) are characters from a Disney show called "Gravity Falls" and the chatbot was created on Shapes.inc. The creator will not be monetised with the chatbots, due to copyright issues involving Disney Enterprises Inc. and the original creator of the show Alex Hirsch, following DMCA
